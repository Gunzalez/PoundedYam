How to use

Anyone can cook these meals!

Pounded Yam is a handy mobile reference to cooking quick and easy Nigerian meals.

Cooking meals

Simply browse to a particular meal, tap on a meal, click on COOK to see details on cooking time, ingredients and best spots to buy these, steps to ensure a delicious dish and common alternatives.

Buying meals

Of course not everyone has the time to do this, so we've also got a list of restaurants serving these meals.

Again on the list of meals, tap on an image, click on BUY and you'll be presented with a list of shops selling that particular meal in a 5 mile radius, depending on you location. You can also pick a different post code if you're ordering for somewhere else.

Other things

You can of course share meals via social media, rate a dish and tell others about it, or just click on the heart symbol to include the meal into your favorites list.
